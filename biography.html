Biography

Hello! This is Sami Mali.

Sami Mali, is a student currently studying B.Sc. Computing (Hons) at IIMS College affiliated by UCSI University. She completed her +2 from Kathmandu Model College, Baghbazar and completed her schooling from Learning Realm International School, Kalanki, Kathmandu. She lives in Chandragiri-15, Kathmandu.
